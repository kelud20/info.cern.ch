Client software

Three developments on the clients side. Tony Johnson of Boston University, developr of the MidasWWW browser for Motif, has ported it now to four X11 platforms and has a tar file of sources available on ftp://info.cern.ch/pub/www/src or ftp://freehep.scri.fsu.edu/freehep/networking_news_email/midaswww as midswww_1.0.tar.Z Tony also has plans to include graphics and text editing in the future.
